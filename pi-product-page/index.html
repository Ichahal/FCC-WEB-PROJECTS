Great For Learning

Whether it is learning Programming, Electronics or Linux Raspberry PI is going to be your best friend through-out your learning journey.

Economical

Raspberry PI is the cheapest full-fledged computer of good quality one can find in the market, everyone's use case for technology is different but everyone loves a product that give you bank for your buck.

Huge Community

Raspberry PI has a welcoming and friendly global community where user's share their latest pi projects that can be anything from making their own personal Cloud storage with pi to building a supercomputer with cluster of pi's.
